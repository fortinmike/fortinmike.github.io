This is the home of a couple of Mac apps I (Michaël Fortin) have created and maintained over the years. The name comes from a personal interest in the Chernobyl disaster and the surrounding exclusion zone (which includes the Pripyat ghost town and the Duga radar array, among others). More recently, in 2019, the excellent Chernobyl mini-series came out. Definitely watch that if you have any interest in the subject!
