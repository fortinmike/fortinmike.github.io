Sleepytime 2, now available on the Mac App Store!

Hi there! Unfortunately Sleepytime 1 has reached the end of its life, but from its ashes rose Sleepytime 2!

Sleepytime 2 sells for 4.99$ on the Mac App Store. As a way to say thanks, all those of you who already donated will receive a free Sleepytime 2 licence! Simply send me an e-mail at fortinmike@irradiated.net using the same e-mail account that you used to make your donation (your PayPal address) and I'll contact you shortly with a promo code to download the app for free on the Mac App Store. Note that this promotion does not apply to those who donate after the release of Sleepytime 2.0 (because I won't have enough promo codes to give away).

Sleepytime 2 offers support for music players other than iTunes, text-to-speech, local weather, a player control bar and multiple custom fullscreen compositions along with the ability to create your own, use them in Sleepytime and share them with other users on the Sleepytime Compositions Gallery, and more. Take a look at all of Sleepytime 2's main features here.

This will be the last update for Sleepytime 1, unless a major bug needs fixing. Sleepytime 1 will remain available for free on Irradiated.net but future support may be limited.

Thanks so much for being a Sleepytime user! I hope you like version 2 as much as I do! :)
