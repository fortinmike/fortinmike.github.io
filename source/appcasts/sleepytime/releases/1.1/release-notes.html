Version 1.1

New Features

  • A new fullscreen mode! You will find fullsceen mode in the View menu or you can press Command-F.
  • Sleepytime can now wake you up too! All-new alarm clock feature with snooze, music fade-in and Apple Remote support.

Bug Fixes

  • Sleepytime now prevents your computer from sleeping before your music stops playing. For example, you can have your music play for 40 minutes even if your computer is set to go to sleep after 15 minutes. No need to change your Energy Saver preferences.
  • Fixed a bug where sometimes the values would not be saved when quitting the app.

Technical Jibba Jabba

  • Refactored much of the code and reduced the amount of code by about 30% (excluding new features of course) (that's a very very good thing).
