Version 2.2
  • Retina graphics & small UI refinements.
  • Added support for the Decibel music player by Sbooth.
  • Replaced the "computer actions" feature with a brand new scripts runner on Mountain Lion and later. You can download a scripts pack from within the app to get the app to put your Mac to sleep and more when the music stops playing.
  • More robust animations rendering, meaning even more stability.
  • Replaced local help with online help for more reliability and always up-to-date help pages.
  • Added opt-in anonymous usage statistics.
  • Fixed: The app was displaying a wrong subtitle when a player wasn't running.
  • Fixed: computer actions (sleep, restart, shutdown, logout) were not working on Mountain Lion due to a change in the Mac OS X sandbox environment.
  • Fixed: The active music player was not detected when waking the computer from sleep.
  • Fixed: The preview alarm music button sometimes did not work after launching the app.
  • Other small tweaks and bug fixes.
